Who am I

Jessy is the founder of Jessy's Journeys. Originally from Albania, I moved to Ireland as a teenager and have spent most of my life there while exploring the world. I have traveled to more than 40 countries across six continents. I believe that everyone should live life to the fullest, and to truly do so, we must disconnect from our daily routines and reconnect with the world around us. Traveling offers the perfect escape allowing us to experience peace, gain new perspectives, and embrace the beauty of different cultures. Inspired by the growth of my Instagram community, I created this website to share must visit destinations that deserve a spot on your bucket list. Travel isn’t just an adventure; it’s a journey that enriches the mind, fuels the soul, and opens doors to unforgettable experiences.
