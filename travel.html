My Top Destinations

New York

New York City, known as "The City That Never Sleeps," is one of the most exciting and dynamic destinations in the world.

Explore the Statue of Liberty, walk through Times Square, and visit Empire State Building or Top of the Rock.

Australia

Sydney the iconic city known for its stunning Opera House, Harbour Bridge, and breathtaking beaches like Bondi and Manly.

While in Australia don’t miss the chance to explore Uluru (Ayers Rock) is a must destination.

Thailand

Bangkok, the vibrant capital of Thailand, is a city full of energy, rich culture, and incredible street food.

While you're in Thailand, don’t miss the chance to visit Phi Phi Islands.

New Zealand

Auckland is the perfect gateway to New Zealand’s natural beauty, culture, and adventure.

While you're in Auckland, a visit to Hobbiton is a must.
